The Real Problem

It Isn't the Surgery. It's the Fifteen Minutes Before It.

Cataract surgery itself is one of the most common, most refined procedures in medicine.

What trips people up is the lens decision that comes with it — and it's permanent. Here's what that can look like:

  • More nighttime glare and halos than you expected
  • Still reaching for reading glasses you were told you might not need
  • Never knowing which questions to ask, because you'd never heard the terms before

That's usually nobody's fault. It's a math problem.

Fifteen minutes is not enough time to absorb words like "toric" and "extended depth of focus" for the first time — and make a decision you'll live with for the rest of your life.

That's the gap ClarityIQ closes — not by telling you what to choose, but by making sure you understand what you're choosing between, before that conversation happens.

Common Questions

Before You Begin

Is this medical advice? +

No. ClarityIQ is an educational conversation, not a diagnosis or treatment recommendation. Nothing here replaces your surgeon's evaluation of your eyes and your medical history — this is meant to prepare you for that conversation, not replace it.

Will it tell me which lens to get? +

No — and that's intentional. We don't have access to your eye exam, your medical history, or the clinical details that go into that decision. What we can do is make sure you understand your options clearly enough that the conversation with your surgeon is easier and more productive.

Is this actually AI? +

Yes. You'll be speaking with a guided AI conversation, not a human. We tell you this clearly before you begin, because we think that's the honest way to do it — not something to bury in fine print.

What happens to what I say? +

Nothing is recorded or stored. No audio, no transcript. Your summary is generated and lives on your device — you choose whether and how to share it.

What if I'm not satisfied? +

The IOL Clarity Session is backed by a guarantee: if you complete the full program and don't come away with a clear, confident summary, you'll be refunded in full within your first 30 days. We'll ask a few questions about what didn't work so we can improve, but that won't hold up your refund. The IOL Clarity Starter doesn't currently include this guarantee.
