The understanding gap

Eligible patients default to the standard lens — not by choice, but by default.

Most cataract patients have never been told that restoring full range vision without glasses is an option. By the time they reach the consultation, they've already anchored on the familiar. The surgeon's job becomes convincing instead of confirming.

How it works

Education before the consultation. Clarity at the chair.

ClarityIQ is a voice-first patient education platform. Patients complete a personalized conversation before their appointment — arriving with a lens preference their surgeon can confirm.

01
Patient receives a link
Before their surgical consultation, the patient receives a text or email with their ClarityIQ link. No app download. No login.
02
Guided voice conversation
ClarityIQ asks about their lifestyle and vision goals, educates them on their options, and answers questions — in plain language, at their pace.
03
Summary to the surgeon
The patient arrives with a lens preference summary. The surgeon reviews an informed patient — not an uninformed one.
