Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

1. Our Approach

ClarityIQ is built to collect as little personal information as possible.

2. What We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly (such as your email address, if given), anonymous usage information about how sessions go, and payment information if you choose to purchase the IOL Clarity Starter or IOL Clarity Session (processed securely by our payment provider — we do not directly collect or store card details).

3. What We Don't Collect

We do not build a health record or profile tied to your identity.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve ClarityIQ — including communicating with you when appropriate and understanding how our tool is being used, so we can make it better over time.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use standard service providers (such as hosting and payment processing) who are bound to protect your information and only use it to provide their service to us.

6. Your Choices

You can request that we delete any information tied to your email address — see Section 8.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the effective date above when we do.

8. Contact

Questions can be directed to support@clarityiq.ai.
